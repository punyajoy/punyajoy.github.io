Biography

Punyajoy Saha is a PhD student at CNeRG Lab in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, West Bengal. Currently, he is doing research in the area of social computing and natural language processing domain under the supervision of Prof. Animesh Mukherjee and Prof. Pawan Goyal. Apart from research, he also loves playing guitar and writing short stories.
